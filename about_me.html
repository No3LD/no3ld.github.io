About Me

I am a new graduate of the University of Illinois – Urbana-Champaign (May 2025) and am one of the first students to graduate with UIUC’s new Computer Science + Animal Sciences program. All four years of my undergraduate studies, I worked as an undergraduate researcher in Dr. Isabella Condotta’s Lab for the Precision Management of Animals, where I learned to apply computer vision and machine learning (ML) to agriculture, livestock, and conservation. I am currently working as an animal trainer while I seek full-time positions to further develop my skills in software development and ML with applications in agriculture, conservation, and biotechnology.
